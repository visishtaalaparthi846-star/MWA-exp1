Welcome to R.V.R.&J.C. College of Engineering Online Bookstore

About the Website

This is an online bookstore for R.V.R.&J.C. College of Engineering students. Browse through our collection of textbooks and reference materials for various departments.

How to Use

1. Browse: Click on your department from the left navigation menu to view available books.

2. Login: Create an account or login to add books to your cart.

3. Register: New users can register for an account.

4. Catalogue: View all available books with details including title, author, publisher, and price.

5. Cart: Review and manage your selected books.

Available Departments

  • CSBS - Computer Science and Business Systems
  • CSE - Computer Science and Engineering
  • ECE - Electronics and Communication Engineering
  • EEE - Electrical and Electronics Engineering
  • CIVIL - Civil Engineering
